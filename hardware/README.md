# Hardware Designs & Schematics

This directory is dedicated to the physical hardware design of the Electrodynamic Dust Shield (EDS). 

Contributors: Please upload your hardware design files here, including:
- **Gerber Files** (typically packaged as a `.zip` archive)
- **Schematic & Layout Images** (renders, screenshots, or SVGs of the PCB layout)
- **Bill of Materials (BOM)** and physical electrode specification sheets
